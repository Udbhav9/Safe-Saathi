Our Color Coded Alert System

Recognize situations at various levels of severity and act effectively.

Red Alert: For imminent danger. Notify emergency contacts and authorities immediately.
Orange Alert: For suspicious activity. Send alerts to your family and friends.
Yellow Alert: For cautionary measures. Record incidents and add GPS location to ensure.
Green Alert: All safe. Save all previous alerts and notifications.

Key Features

Alert & Sonic Shield System

In extreme cases (Red), it sends an SOS with live location and emergency details. For potential threats (Orange), it alerts trusted contacts. At low risk (Yellow), it provides safety tools, and when safe (Green), it tracks location without alerts unless triggered

Virtual Legal Desk

Combination of legal support and real-time safety information. It allows users to file police complaints or legal notices directly through the app, either independently or with help from a virtual assistant. Users can track the status of their complaints and legal cases, with real-time updates

Legal & Safety AI Companion

Offers users real-time, AI-driven chatbot assistance for legal and safety needs. Users can ask legal questions in everyday language and receive actionable steps in return. The system provides advice on laws, rights, and filing complaints, with support available in regional languages

Anonymized Reporting

Allows users to report crimes or incidents without revealing their identity. It offers an anonymous reporting option that routes reports directly to NGOs, police, or community leaders for prompt action
